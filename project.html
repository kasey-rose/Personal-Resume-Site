Exploration of Ukraine-Russia War Through Twitter, Team Leader

May 2024
  • Using word counting, topic modeling, and classification in Python as well as packages, including “pandas”, “seaborn”, “matplotlip”, and “tidytext” to analyze tweets about the Russia-Ukraine conflict to figure out how the public perceives the Russo-Ukrainian War
  • Our goal was to see the public perception of the war over some time, extract recurring themes, such as support for Ukraine, gain a deeper understanding of the conflict itself, and to create data visualizations to demonstrate our goal
